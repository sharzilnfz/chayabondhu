A Seamless, Trust-Based Ecosystem

Our model is built on a foundation of trust, quality, and accessibility, creating a reliable link between those who need care and those who provide it.

The Chhayabondhu Model:

  • 1

    Families Connect: Clients use our user-friendly website or call center to define their specific care needs.

  • 2

    Platform Matches: Our system identifies the best-suited caregiver based on skills, experience, and client preferences.

  • 3

    Hospitals Train: We partner with reputable hospitals to provide certified, disease-specific training, ensuring high standards.

  • 4

    Care Delivered: A vetted, trained, and empathetic caregiver provides professional care in the comfort of the client's home.

Our Target Audience

Our service is designed for the patient (User) but purchased and managed by their family (Buyer), primarily middle-income working adults.

Flexible Services & Unmatched Quality

We offer adaptable service models and ensure our caregivers are uniquely qualified through a rigorous training and vetting process.

Service Delivery Options

Hourly & Part-Time Support

Ideal for families needing assistance for specific hours during the day. Caregivers work shifts and return home, providing support for daily tasks like bathing, feeding, medication reminders, and mobility assistance.

Full-Time & Live-In Support

Designed for clients who require round-the-clock care and companionship. Caregivers stay with the family full-time for the contract duration, ensuring continuous, dedicated support.
